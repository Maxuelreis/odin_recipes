Pudim de Leite Moça (Milk Pudding)

The “Pudim de Leite Moça” is a classic homemade dessert recipe and one of the most popular desserts in Brazil. As the name implies, the main ingredient is sweetened condensed milk.

Ingredients:

For the caramel sauce:

  • 200g sugar
  • 50ml water

For the pudding:

  • 300ml sweetened condensed milk
  • 300ml milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • ring mold (1,2 litres)

Preparation:

Preparation of the caramel sauce:

1 - Melt the sugar over low heat in a saucepan.

2 - When melted sugar turns golden in color, add the water and boil. When all the melted sugar is dissolved the caramel is ready to line the mold. This caramel may be stored and used in other desserts such as “panna cotta”.

3 - Another faster way to make caramel is to melt 150 g sugar into the ring mold over low heat until golden brown, turning the mold to spread the caramel evenly. Using a fork makes it easier to spread caramel evenly.

4 - You may also make individual portions.

Enjoy it!
